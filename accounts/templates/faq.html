How often is the data refreshed?

The data is refreshed about once per week, but if you want your profile on FediDevs.com updated sooner, send us an email and we'll update it manually.

How to get removed from FediDevs.com?

The easiest way is to mark your account as not discoverable in the Mastodon profile settings. Uncheck the checkbox that says "Suggest account to others" at {your_instance}/settings/profile. This will remove your account the next time we sync.

If you don't want to wait until the next sync please reach out and we'll remove you immediately.
